Health

A Chairs and Tables Theme (along with Happiness and Home)

  • 01. Paul "Ftrain" Ford

Every year Chairs and Tables picks a theme to research for at least four seasons. We then start writing a report on it. They take a while to get finished but ... we're working on 'em. It was a coincidence that all of our themes so far have begun with an 'h'. Happiness, Home and now Health.

For 2015 the theme is 'Health' and instead of biking across a country or individually meeting people at their homes, we've asked people to respond to the prompt:

what good health means to you and how you maintain/strive-towards it.

The editorial team consists of Asad Chishti, Caroline Crocker, and Andrew Pitkin. With support from Jonathon Reed and a collaboration with Lorenzo Colocado whose project Rise over Run, in part, inspired this.

If you'd be interested in submitting something or reaching out, send Asad an email.
