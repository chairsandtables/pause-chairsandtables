Who We Are

The best way to get a chair at the table is to build one yourself.

Asad Chishti and Jonathon Reed are explorers who met in the city of Kingston.

Born in the Fall, a few years and several thousand kilometers apart, following their twin sisters; Asad is passionate about potlucks, maps, and naps, and Jonathon about breaking down gender norms, ukuleles, and more. Between the two of them they've worked on projects like the Occupy Movement, Idle No More, Humans of Kingston, Vanhawks, TEDxQueensU, Awesome Kingston, Queen's Journal, Jack.org, Ontario Youth Parliament, Adjacent Possibilities, and MaRSDD.
