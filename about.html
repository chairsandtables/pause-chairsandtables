Who We Are

​
​
​

The best way to get a chair at the table is to build one yourself.

​

Asad Chishti and Jonathon Reed are explorers.

​

Born in the fall, a few years and several thousand kilometers apart, following their twin sisters, they ended up at the same university. Asad is passionate about potlucks, maps, and naps; and Jonathon about wilderness, harmonicas, and hot chocolate. Between the two of them they've worked on projects with/for Humans of Kingston, Awesome Kingston, TEDxQueensU, Queen’s Journal, CFRC, Vanhawks, Jack.org, Adjacent Possibilities, MaRSDD, Centre for Social Innovation, Artscape, Coffeee Public, Ontario Youth Parliament, Samara Canada, 350.org, and more. (Yes, we realize that’s an obscenely long list. We're working on it.)
