As the Raven Flies

A question of now and where.

In an attempt to add context to Canada’s history for the 150th anniversary of Confederation, we plan to digitally re-map ‘Canada’ through a 15,000-km, 6-month bicycle tour from the Atlantic Ocean to the Pacific Ocean via the Arctic.

Annual Research

What does it mean to live a good life?

Since inception, we’ve picked an annual theme to research for four seasons and compile a report on. This is the North Star with which CHAT operates and defines annual objectives. These are generally vague enough to fit ~anything.

Immersive Documentation

Micro to Macro, Urban to Rural

Within our individual work and projects throughout the past few years, we’ve gotten very good at capturing widely and deeply. Particularly probing with our questions and cameras but mostly with our practice of collecting markers throughout an event or day.

What we Do

We get asked this a lot, and the answer is often varied and context-
dependent. The simplest answer is we do whatever you need.

  • Visual Communication

    We’re highly experienced in photography and videography in a variety of contexts: quickly, slowly; landscapes, people.

  • Facilitation

    We can organize events, conferences, forums. We have experience in leading both small workshops and national-level programs, conferences, gatherings.

  • Research

    Interviewing, documentation, and being ethnographers. We can communicate the data and stories in a powerful way. A rare breed of artsy scientists, and sciency artists.

  • Design

    We believe in building things ourselves as much as possible, which means we’ve developed professional-level skills in graphic design and website development.

  • Digital Support

    We can augment your digital strategies and tactics with technological duct tape. Just about anything with computers, we either know where to look or how to do it.

  • Collaboration

    Nearly all of our work depends on meaningful and constructive collaboration. We are more than adept at building connections between organizations and people.
